# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability in EYDII, please report it responsibly.

**Email:** [security@veritera.ai](mailto:security@veritera.ai)

Please include:

- A description of the vulnerability
- Steps to reproduce the issue
- The potential impact
- Any suggested remediation

## Response Timeline

- **Acknowledgement:** Within 48 hours
- **Initial assessment:** Within 5 business days
- **Resolution target:** Based on severity

## Scope

This policy covers:

- EYDII SDKs (JavaScript, Python)
- Framework adapters in this repository
- The EYDII platform at id.veritera.ai

## Responsible Disclosure

- Do not publicly disclose the vulnerability before we have addressed it
- Do not access, modify, or delete data belonging to other users
- Do not perform actions that could degrade the service for others

## Out of Scope

- Social engineering attacks against Veritera employees
- Denial-of-service attacks
- Issues in third-party dependencies (report these upstream)

## Recognition

We appreciate security researchers who help keep EYDII and its users safe. With your permission, we will acknowledge your contribution.
